About SJP

Built by the People,
For the People

Sampoorna Janta Party (SJP) was founded with a singular, unshakeable belief — that every citizen of this great nation deserves equal opportunity, justice, and dignity. We are not a party of promises. We are a movement of action.

🎯
Our Vision
A self-reliant, prosperous, and inclusive India where no citizen is left behind.
💡
Our Mission
Policy-driven governance focused on education, employment, healthcare, and sustainability.
Justice & Equality for every citizen
Transparent and Accountable Governance
Sustainable Development & Environment
National Pride with Constitutional Values
Inclusive Growth — No One Left Behind

Our Manifesto

A Clear Agenda for
A Better India

Our seven-pillar agenda is rooted in ground realities and guided by the aspiration of every Indian citizen.

Education for All
Quality education as a fundamental right, not a privilege.
Free education till class 12
Skill development in every district
Digital classrooms in rural schools
Employment
Creating 2 crore dignified jobs in 5 years.
MSME support and startup fund
Government job drive in rural areas
Apprenticeship with top industries
Healthcare
Universal healthcare — no family left without care.
Free treatment for BPL families
Primary health centers in all villages
Affordable medicines policy
Infrastructure
Modern infrastructure connecting every corner of India.
Roads, bridges and smart cities
High-speed internet in rural areas
Clean drinking water for all
Agriculture
Doubling farmer income through modern, fair agriculture.
MSP guarantee for all crops
Crop insurance reform
Irrigation in every district
Women Empowerment
Safety, opportunity, and leadership for women.
33% reservation in legislatures
Zero tolerance for gender crime
Women entrepreneur fund
Environment
Green India — a legacy for future generations.
100% renewable energy by 2035
Plant 10 crore trees program
Clean river and air mission
